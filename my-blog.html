My Tech Journey

Published on June 30, 2026 by Abdulrahim Hassan

My tech journey started in school with Java in 200-level, then JavaScript and PHP in 300-level. I even used Java to build a Resource Management System for my final year project.

After graduation, I was determined to go deeper into web development, but couldn’t access free training and didn’t have funds for paid programs. I kept applying for opportunities, including eHealth Africa Academy.

I’ve now been accepted into the UX/UI Design phase with eHealth Africa Academy. It’s my first time in design, and balancing it with limited time is tough. But I’m here, I’m learning, and I’m not stopping.

Clean structure always precedes beautiful design.
